Collections overview

Anthracology

Permanent charcoal and wood reference collection, with ongoing digitization of taxonomic and contextual records.

Carpology

Permanent seed reference collection designed to interoperate with anthracology through structured inventories and imaging.

Palynology

Pollen and spore reference collection with existing photo documentation and progressive structuring of analytical data.

Traceology

Experimental lithic trace collection documented by photography, with high-resolution and microscopy workflows being standardized.

Lithotheque (LithIPHES)

Lithic raw material reference collection with photography and initial 3D capture, valorized through LithIPHES v1.0.

Microscopy

Archive of microscopy images being centralized with harmonized formats, instrument metadata, and long-term preservation.

Photography Slides

Scientific photography and slide archives from multiple authors, currently being digitized and indexed with shared metadata.

Zooarchaeology (COR-IPHES)

Advanced FAIR-aligned osteological reference collection with online 3D models and an operational PostgreSQL database.

Access

Zooarchaeology (TaphoIPHES)

Experimental zooarchaeology collection from taphonomic studies, currently photo-documented with further 3D and data structuring planned.

Micropalaeontology

Permanent reference collection of bones, teeth, and related elements with ongoing digitization and photographic coverage.

Palaeoanthropology

Reference collection of cranial and post-cranial replicas with partial digitization combining 3D scanning and photography.

Technotheque

Teaching-oriented experimental lithic typology collection progressing toward maturity levels comparable to LithIPHES and COR-IPHES.

Microwear

Reference collection of dental moulds used in biomarker studies, currently managed in Excel with digitization in progress.

Archaeopalaeontology

Temporary fossil collection managed through FileMaker and Excel, with ongoing photographic digitization and standardized inventory fields.
