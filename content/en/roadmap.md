# Provisional schedule

## Year 1 - Structuring
- Information gathering.
- Definition of priorities across collections.
- Selection of tools (software, standards, protocols).
- Integration by design: open science, FAIR, ISO standards, ontologies.
- Protocol development.
- Initial implementation on a limited number of collections.
- Process adjustments based on field feedback.
- Start of dissemination and visibility activities focused on protocols.

## Year 2 - Deployment
- Recruitment of two specialist technicians.
- Training of the two specialist technicians.
- Progressive rollout of processes across multiple collections.
- Implementation and stabilization of tools and protocols.
- Process adjustments based on field feedback.
- Scientific and institutional dissemination and visibility.

## Year 3 - Consolidation
- Full documentation (procedures, reference materials, resources).
- External positioning (networks, inter-laboratory collaborations).
- Sustainability of the scheme.
- Scientific and institutional dissemination and visibility.

## Overview
The three phases (Structuring, Deployment, Consolidation) operationalize the strategic logic of WP1 and WP2 over 2026-2028.
